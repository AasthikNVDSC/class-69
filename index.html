About us


Indu International School (IIS) is a Premier International School, established in 2011, to form competent and committed leaders who are ethical, principle-centred and socially responsible with a global perspective and entrepreneurial spirit. IIS is managed by Miracle Jesus Education Society well-known for its outstanding contribution to education. IIS stands for excellence and ethics which are the hallmarks of the school and all its programmes and activities embody these two elements. IIS earnestly strives to inculcate in the students the values of excellence, justice, honesty and service to the society. IIS concentrates on the holistic formation of students in all aspects viz., intellectual skills, social, emotional and spiritual. The school works with an undeterred zeal to offer its students the best education, blending classroom and experiential learning.
